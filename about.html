About Axis Tours and Travel

Creating extraordinary East African travel experiences

Our Story

Axis Tours and Travel began with a simple mission: to create extraordinary travel experiences across East Africa that go beyond the ordinary. What started as a passion for exploring Kenya's beautiful coastline and wildlife has grown into a trusted name in East African travel.

Our founder, Alex Etidit, had a vision of making authentic East African experiences accessible while maintaining the highest standards of service and local knowledge. Today, we continue to uphold those values while serving travelers from around the world.

We believe that travel should be transformative, inspiring, and unforgettable. Every journey we plan is crafted with attention to detail, ensuring that our clients experience East Africa's most beautiful destinations in the most authentic way possible.
