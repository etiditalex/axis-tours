Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For East African safaris and beach holidays, we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance, especially for peak seasons and popular destinations. However, we can accommodate last-minute requests for select properties.

What is included in your packages?

Our packages typically include accommodations, airport transfers, daily meals, guided tours, and 24/7 support. Additional services like luxury upgrades and special experiences can be customized based on your preferences.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive travel insurance options tailored for East African travel, including coverage for safari equipment, medical emergencies, and trip cancellation.

Can you arrange custom itineraries?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating bespoke East African travel experiences. Our travel experts work closely with you to design the perfect itinerary for your specific needs and preferences.
