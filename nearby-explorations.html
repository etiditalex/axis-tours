Explore Beyond Mombasa

Coastal Kenya's Extended Wonders

Discover the diverse landscapes and experiences just beyond the city limits

While Mombasa offers incredible experiences, the surrounding coastal region holds even more treasures waiting to be discovered. From the artistic haven of Kilifi to the pristine marine sanctuary of Wasini Island, these nearby destinations offer unique perspectives on coastal Kenya's natural beauty and cultural richness.

Kilifi

A serene coastal town north of Mombasa with kite-surfing and ecological artistry

Just north of Mombasa lies the peaceful coastal town of Kilifi, a haven for adventure seekers and nature lovers. This serene destination offers world-class kite-surfing conditions, breathtaking ecological artistry including the spectacular carmine bee-eater murmuration, and ancient ruins of Gedi that tell stories of centuries past.

What Makes Kilifi Special:

  • World-class kite-surfing conditions
  • Carmine bee-eater murmuration
  • Ancient Gedi ruins
  • Creative venues like "Salty's on the Creek"

Popular Activities:

  • Kite-surfing lessons and equipment rental
  • Bird watching and photography tours
  • Historical tours of Gedi ruins
  • Cultural performances and art exhibitions

Wasini Island / Kisite Mpunguti Marine Park

A peaceful, car-free island perfect for dolphin tours and coral snorkeling

Escape to the tranquility of Wasini Island, a car-free paradise within the Kisite Mpunguti Marine Park. This peaceful island offers unforgettable dolphin tours, snorkeling over vibrant coral reefs, and immersive cultural visits that connect you with the local community. Experience the perfect blend of marine adventure and cultural authenticity.

What Makes Wasini Special:

  • Car-free island experience
  • Dolphin watching tours
  • Vibrant coral reef snorkeling
  • Immersive cultural experiences

Popular Activities:

  • Boat tours to dolphin hotspots
  • Guided snorkeling adventures
  • Local village visits and cultural immersion
  • Fresh seafood dining experiences

Getting There & Logistics

Transportation

Comfortable private transfers from Mombasa to Kilifi (1 hour) and Wasini Island (2 hours).

Duration

Best Time to Visit

June to October for optimal weather. Kite-surfing best during trade wind season.

What to Bring
